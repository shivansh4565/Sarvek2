about us

At Sarvek for Social Welfare,

our mission is to foster compassion and support for marginalized communities, including the elderly, teenage mothers, domestic violence survivors, and others in need.

We aim to:

Supporting Elderly Care: Connect with old age homes, host events, and facilitate donations to improve the quality of life for senior citizens.

Empowering Teenage Mothers: Provide emotional support, education, and resources for teenage mothers who face social ostracization, promoting their well-being and integration into society.

Supporting Domestic Violence Survivors: Volunteer efforts and legal aid to support domestic violence survivors, ensuring they have access to the resources needed for recovery and justice.Encouraging

"Empowering Teen Mothers: Breaking the Cycle of Education Barriers"

Explore the challenges faced by teen mothers, particularly those from uneducated families forced into early marriages, and the importance of supporting their education.

*Barriers Faced by Teen Mothers:* Discuss the societal stigma, economic challenges, and lack of support networks that hinder teen mothers from continuing their education. - *Importance of Education:* Highlight the transformative impact of education on breaking cycles of poverty and empowering young mothers to build better futures.

- *Support Programs:* Showcase initiatives and organizations offering educational support, childcare assistance, and mentoring to teen mothers.

*Conclusion:* Advocate for policy changes and community support systems that promote educational opportunities and empowerment for teen mothers.

Honoring Wisdom: Case Studies in Caring for Elderly People in India"

Explore successful initiatives and cultural practices that promote the well-being and dignity of elderly individuals in India.

Traditional Care Practices: Discuss cultural norms and family structures that traditionally support elderly care in India, such as multigenerational households and community involvement.

Challenges in Modern Times:Highlight contemporary challenges faced by elderly individuals, including social isolation, healthcare access, and financial insecurity.

Innovative Care Models: Showcase examples of organizations and communities implementing innovative care models, such as senior centers, home healthcare services, and intergenerational programs.

Conclusion:* Emphasize the importance of honoring and supporting elderly individuals through policies and practices that promote dignity, quality of life, and community engagement.

2th june, 2024 by Sreehitha
"Breaking the Silence: Domestic Violence in India and Cultural Perspectives"

Explore the prevalence of domestic violence in India, despite its low reported divorce rates, and its impact on individuals and communities.

Understanding Domestic Violence: Provide an overview of domestic violence statistics, types of abuse, and factors contributing to its persistence in Indian society.

Cultural Norms and Barriers: Discuss how cultural norms, patriarchal structures, and economic dependence perpetuate cycles of abuse and discourage reporting and seeking help.

Support and Legal Framework: Highlight initiatives, laws, and support services available for domestic violence survivors, including shelters, counseling, and legal aid.

Conclusion: Call for societal awareness, policy reforms, and community support systems that empower survivors, challenge harmful norms, and prevent domestic violence.
